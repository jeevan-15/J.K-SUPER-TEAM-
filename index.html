Our History

The JK SUPER TEAM was founded by Jeevan Singh on July 1, 2019. Over the years, the team has seen multiple changes in size, but it currently consists of 7 dedicated members. On August 19, 2019, a new company, KOGA™®, emerged from JK SUPER TEAM, marking a successful expansion. Unfortunately, the rise of the KOGA company was cut short by the COVID-19 pandemic, and it closed in March 2020. This caused significant challenges for both companies. However, JK SUPER TEAM is once again thriving and is excited to announce that it is going online. Our products will soon be listed on the website, and we are looking forward to offering online services in the near future.
